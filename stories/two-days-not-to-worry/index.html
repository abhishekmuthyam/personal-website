🗓️ Two Days We Should Never Worry About

← Work & Life Stories

A man could not sleep for many nights. Tension at work, tension at home, tension about money. He went to a wise old woman in his town and said, 'Worry is eating me alive.'

The old woman said, 'Let me tell you a secret. There are two days in every week you should never worry about.'

'The first day is yesterday. Its mistakes, its pains, its missed chances — yesterday has gone forever. All the money in the world cannot bring back even one minute of it. You cannot undo a single word you spoke.'

'The second day is tomorrow. Its problems, its dangers, its promises — tomorrow is not in your hands yet. The sun will rise tomorrow whether you worry tonight or sleep in peace.'

'That leaves only one day — today. And here is the truth: any person can fight the battle of just one day.'

'We break down not because of today. We break down when we carry yesterday's regret and tomorrow's fear on today's shoulders. That load is too heavy for anyone.'

The man went home, and that night, for the first time in months, he slept — holding only one day in his hands.

💛 What this story teaches

Yesterday is gone and tomorrow is not yet born. You only ever have to handle one day — today.
